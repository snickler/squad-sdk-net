# Team Ceremonies

## 1. Design Review
**Trigger:** Before any multi-agent task begins  
**Purpose:** Agree on approach before parallel work starts  
**Format:**
1. Lead presents the task and proposed approach
2. Each agent states what they'll do
3. Conflicts or overlaps are resolved
4. Lead confirms assignments

## 2. Retrospective
**Trigger:** After any failed or reverted task  
**Purpose:** Identify root cause and prevent recurrence  
**Format:**
1. What happened?
2. Why did it happen?
3. What will we change?
4. Record outcome in decisions.md
